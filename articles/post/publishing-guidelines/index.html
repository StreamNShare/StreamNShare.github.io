Publishing Guidelines

Below are the publishing guidelines for articles on Stream ‘n Share. Please refer to these guidelines if you’re interested in writing for us. Any articles that don’t meet these standards will not be published. For reference, check out any of the articles on the main page.

All articles on Stream ‘n Share will allow the author to link their stream link directly to the article in question for additional exposure like so:

Attention Reader! Enjoy reading this article? Please use the below link to directly support the writer responsible so they can contribute more to Stream 'n Share in the future!

Twitch Link: https://twitch.tv/TheChiefMeat
Support Link: https://www.paypal.me/TheChiefMeat

  • Ensure that all articles are written to the best of your ability without personal bias. If you’d like to include a personal view on a topic, please keep it succinct and on point. This section of the article will be included at the bottom of the article page and will be clearly separated.

  • Ensure you have corrected all grammatical and spelling errors to the best of your ability.

  • When referenced, include links to subject matter.

  • Ensure to include a working title for your article.

  • Please include the date of when the article was submitted.

  • If possible, please format your article as Markdown (this will be done for you if you cannot).

  • Include your streaming link (Twitch/YouTube/Beam) with the written article.

  • Any articles that require major edits will show the previous text and the current updated text like so (this does not include spelling or grammatical corrections):

Attention Reader! The below article has been edited! Please use the below information to see what text in this article was edited by the writer so you can stay informed.

+ This text was added.

- This text was removed.

  • Any working relationship with developers or publishers must be disclosed and included with your article.

  • When reviewing a game that has been provided to you by the developers or publisher, this fact must be disclosed and included with your article.

Once you have finished writing an article, please use the following link to submit your work and provide a working email address for the editor to get back to you. Stream ‘n Share reserves the right not to publish your work.

Stream ‘n Share Article Submissions: https://fncontact.com/SNSArticles
