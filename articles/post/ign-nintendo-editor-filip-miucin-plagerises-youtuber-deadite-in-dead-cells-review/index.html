IGN Nintendo Editor Filip Miucin Plagerises YouTuber Deadite in Dead Cells Review

August 07, 2018
Gaming News

IGN Nintendo Editor Filip Miucin recently published his video review for Dead Cells, his first ever video review for the site. YouTuber Deadite, who hosts the YouTube channel Boomstick Gaming, found that the video review in question was eerily similar to his own review. Below is a side by side comparsion by Deadite, showcasing the most egregious plagerisms, as well as a tweet from Philip Miucin:

Finishing up my Dead Cells review this weekend. It’s the first IGN video review I’ve edited myself, and I can’t wait to share it with you all. If you still haven’t seen Dead Cells in action, check out the first 21 minutes on Nintendo Switch!
Video: https://t.co/xIwXGxKlDv pic.twitter.com/0Ympm06Rx6

— Filip Miucin (@FilipMiucin) August 5, 2018

IGN responded with:

“As a group of writers and creators who value our own work and that of others in our field, the editorial staff of IGN takes plagiarism very seriously. In light of concerns that have been raised about our Dead Cells review, we’ve removed it for the time being and are investigating.”

Article Update (12th of August 2018)

IGN have completed their investigation and issued the following video statement, as well as the following statement on Twitter:
