Square Enix Pulls Mobile Games From Belgium

November 26, 2018
Gaming News

Square Enix has decided to pull three mobile games from Belgium. Recently Belgiums gaming commission concluded that loot boxes were in direct violation of their gambling laws.

The three games are:

Mobius Final Fantasy

Kingdom Hearts Union X

Dissidia Final Fantasy Opera Omnia
