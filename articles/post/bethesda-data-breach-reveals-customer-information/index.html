Bethesda Data Breach Reveals Customer Information (Updated)

December 06, 2018
Gaming News

After a trialling week, Bethesda has once again found themselves in hot water. Their support page for Fallout 76 Power Armor Edition users was found to be publicly leaking sensitive user information, including receipts which could contain emails, home addresses and even the card used to purchase the game.

So far, Bethesda has yet to respond on any public channels, only through a forum reply saying “Hi guys, we’ve resolved this issue.”
