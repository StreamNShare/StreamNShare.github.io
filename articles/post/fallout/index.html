Fallout 76 Beta Missing Client Side Authentication Making Cheating Easy

November 06, 2018
Gaming News

Fallout 76 has already become the target of hackers, despite only recenting enter beta. wallhax.com shows off a rather feature rich tool including a 2D radar, field of view correction, stick to target fuction as well as other features, which they claim will be “available at launch”.

Reddit user teetharejustdone detailed how the game fails to verify the intergity of game files, leading to the ability to mod the game and potentially use the engines feature to exploit game mechanics.

Bethesda has replied with the following statement:

“Many of the claims in the thread are either inaccurate or based on incorrect assumptions. The community has however called to attention several issues that our teams are already actively tracking and planning to roll out fixes for. Our goal is always to deliver a great experience for all our players. Cheating or hacking will not be tolerated. We know our fan base is passionate about modding and customizing their experience in our worlds and it’s something we intend to support down the road.”
